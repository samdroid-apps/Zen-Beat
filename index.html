Test your musical memory skills with a totally new kind of game!

Zen Beat is easy to learn but provides endless challenging levels.

Simply listen to the tune, remember the notes and press the corresponding icons. At first, the game is easy with 2 different notes and 3 notes per tune. But it gets progressively harder: 8 different notes and over 60 notes per tune.
